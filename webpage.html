ilahia college of engineering and technology

lahia College of Engineering and Technology (ICET), established in 2002 by the Ilahia Trust, is a private engineering college in Mulavoor, Muvattupuzha, in the Ernakulam district of Kerala. It is affiliated with the APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University (KTU) and approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE).
