Department of computer application

lahia College of Engineering and Technology (ICET) offers a Master of Computer Applications (MCA) program, established in 2008, which focuses on preparing students for careers in the IT industry and emphasizes student-centric learning, industry exposure, and career development in computer applications, including areas like Artificial Intelligence and Cyber Security. The department aims to foster innovation and provide a platform for students to develop practical skills through hands-on experience, industry projects, and collaborations with professionals.
